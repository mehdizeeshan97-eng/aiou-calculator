About Us

The AIOU Result Calculator is a simple tool designed to help students of Allama Iqbal Open University calculate their final results based on assignments, quizzes, and paper marks. Our aim is to make result calculation easier, accurate, and accessible for all AIOU students.

Terms of Use

This calculator is developed for educational purposes only. While every effort is made to ensure accuracy, the final official result is subject to the Allama Iqbal Open University’s announcement. By using this calculator, you agree that we are not responsible for any discrepancies between the calculated and official results.

Privacy Policy

The AIOU Result Calculator does not collect or store any personal information from users. All data entered (assignments, quiz, paper marks) remains only on your device and is not saved or shared with anyone.
